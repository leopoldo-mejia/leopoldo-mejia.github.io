NanoQuantum Lab at UNAB

We combine theory and computations to investigate the fundamental principles that govern the quantum behavior of molecules, nanostructures, and materials. We focus on the use of nonconventional theoretical frameworks, such us many-body Green's functions and stochastic techniques, to develop new methods and physical approximations to unsderstand the electronic structure and dynamics of systems under nonquilibrium conditions, systems driven by external stimuli, open quantum systems, chiral-induced phenomena, and more!
